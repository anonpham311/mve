Positioning Statement

Mission

Help millions of foreigners speak Vietnamese and deliver sustainable value to all our learners.

Vision

Diversify teaching program, gradually improve teaching quality to meet the needs of Vietnamese-language learners. We aim to become the best choice for foreigners to learn Vietnamese.

Core values

Study goes as a pair with practice. Learners above all else. ‘Caring’, ‘understanding’, ‘responsible’ and ‘professional’ are what we put first to treat our learners.

Help us to “make Vietnamese easier”

Your donation can help us to develop the website and produce more high-quality video lessons in attempt to bring Vietnamese to the world.
